Frequently asked questions

If you have other questions, just ask
What should I have ready for class?

Basic cookware, a chopping board, knives, stove, and pantry staples. I’ll send a detailed list and prep notes 48 hours prior.

Do you cater to dietary needs?

Yes — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, and other allergies can be accommodated. Please share details in your booking.

Can you provide ingredients?

Yes. I can source ingredients and bring them along. This adds a per-person cost depending on the menu.

What is the rescheduling policy?

Free reschedule up to 24 hours before. Within 24 hours, a 50% reschedule fee may apply to cover prep and purchases.

How do online classes work?

You’ll receive a secure link and a shopping list. We cook together step-by-step. A recording can be shared on request.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Absolutely. Digital gift cards are available for any amount or specific class packs.
